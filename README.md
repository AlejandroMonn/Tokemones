Tokemon is a game where you can figth against other mokepons with your own, you have 3 seleccions of attacks and these work like a rock papaer scissors logic 

to play mokepon the goal is simple select your partner find the enemy on the map and win the turn-based battle that will ensue

it all starts on the pet selection screen here you must choose one of the three available mokepons hipodoge capipepo or ratigueya be sure to press the select button to confirm your choice
if you forget to do so you will see a warning asking you to choose your friend before continuing


once your pet is ready you will enter the map section now its time to explore control your character using the arrow keys on your keyboard or the buttons that appear on the screen
your only goal at this stage is to find and collide with your opponents pet which will be visible on the map the moment your paths cross the game will immediately activate the turn-based
combat phase and the battle will begin



If you want to play it oflline open the index.html and run it chosee the option oflline and play with the mouse or with your keyboard
